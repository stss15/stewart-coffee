Quick Start

The Shift-Up Rule

Low-fat plant milks lack dairy's surfactants. Ignore Settings 1 & 2!

  • Setting 3 — Lattes & Flat Whites
  • Setting 4/5 — Cappuccinos, Cold Foam & Frappés

Soy Tempering Protocol

To prevent curdling, add 20ml soy milk to your cup before brewing coffee. This buffers the acidity.

Customisation Tips

  • Syrups (Vanilla, Caramel, etc.) can be added to any drink
  • Brown sugar is brewed onto to create a syrup base
