The Ninja Luxe Café Pro: Plant-Based Barista CompendiumOperational Guide for Low-Fat Oat & Soy Formulations (E701)1.0 Operational FundamentalsThe Shift-Up Rule: Low-fat plant milks lack the surfactants of dairy. Ignore Setting 1 and 2.Use Setting 3 for Lattes/Flat Whites.Use Setting 4/5 for Cappuccinos, Cold Foam, and Frappés.The Soy Tempering Protocol: To prevent curdling, add 20ml of soy milk to your cup before brewing the coffee to buffer the acidity.Customisation: Syrups (Vanilla, Caramel, etc.) can be added to any staple. Brown sugar is treated as a textural ingredient and is brewed onto to create a syrup base.Ninja E701 Reference ChartMachine SettingCoffee OutputSuggested Milk (ml)Small (S)180 ml100 - 120 mlMedium (M)235 ml150 - 180 mlMedium+ (M+)295 ml200 - 220 ml2.0 The Staples: Standard Daily TemplatesPure daily drivers. Brew "Rich" directly into the paste.3.2 The Toffee-Nut "System Override"Mode: Double Espresso.Milk: Setting 4 (Max) | 180 ml.Additives: 2 pumps Sugar-Free Toffee Nut.Logic: Uses the Max Froth setting to add "faux-body" to thin oat milk for a latte-style drink.3.3 The Maple-Soy CortadoMode: Single Espresso.Milk: Setting 3 | 60 ml.Additives: 1 tsp Sugar-Free Maple Syrup.4.0 Specialty Over Ice & Cold Brew4.1 The Brown Sugar Shaken EspressoMode: Over Ice - Medium (M).Milk: Setting 5 (Cold Froth) | 120 ml.Additives: 2 tbsp Brown Sugar + Ice.Method: Brew hot concentrate onto sugar. Shake with ice for 15s. Top with cold foam.4.2 The "Zero-G" Vanilla Cold BrewMode: Cold Brew - Medium+ (M+).Milk: Setting 5 | 100 ml.Additives: 1 tbsp Sugar-Free Vanilla.4.3 The Salted Caramel Cold-PressedMode: Cold Pressed Espresso (Double).Milk: Setting 5 | 150 ml.Additives: 1 pump Sugar-Free Caramel + pinch of Sea Salt.5.0 Specialty Frappés & Blended DrinksRequires an external blender (e.g., Ninja Nutri-Blender) for final processing.5.1 The "Velvet" Oat FrappéMode: Double Espresso.Milk: Setting 5 (Cold Froth) | 150 ml.Additives: 1 pump Sugar-Free Syrup + 1 cup Ice.Method: Brew espresso and allow to cool slightly. Add cooled coffee, milk, syrup, and ice to blender. Blend for 20-30s until smooth. The Cold Froth milk provides the emulsification needed for a creamy finish.5.2 The Soya Mocha FrappéMode: Double Espresso.Milk: Setting 5 | 150 ml.Additives: 1 tbsp Cocoa Powder + 1 tsp Brown Sugar + 1 cup Ice.Method: Dissolve cocoa and sugar in hot espresso.
